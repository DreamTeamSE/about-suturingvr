Research Assistant Instructions


Collection Code Scheme

Study Coordinators will notify you of collection codes for that day, general scheme is as follows.

Oncology Ward: OC001, OC002, OC003 …………… OC023…………. OC142


Items Needed

Quest Headset - Turn off completely and put on charger when done with data collection for the day

Two Quest Controllers

Carry Case

Headphones (if desired)

Tablet/Computer - If Android tablet, install Oculus app via Google Play Store. Otherwise get Google Chrome on it.


Process to use Quest VR


Sanitization

  1. Items Included
    1. Oculus Quest Headset
    2. Oculus Quest Controller
    3. Carrying Case
  2. Process of Cleaning VR
    1. Before procedure:
      1. Wipe down VR and controller with Lysol wipe and microfiber cloth
      2. Help patient wear disposable VR liner
    2. After procedure:
      1. Remove and dispose of disposable VR liner
      2. Wipe down VR and controller with Lysol wipe and microfiber cloth
    3. End of Day:
      1. Wipe down VR and controller with Lysol wipe and microfiber cloth
      2. Wipe down VR carrying case with Lysol wipe
